We claim that discarding the photon reference frame as invalid means that we shut our eyes to a way of seeing the world that could bring solutions to some known problems.

Instead we propose to build a theory that only builds on photons frames of reference, and discard any maths about frames of references that are not the speed of light.

The idea is that this collapses space time to a graph with simpler math, but what can we do we it.

In this paper, we attempt to cast 3 well known challenging problems into that setting, and investigate how it brings any new understanding.
